## MCQ 1
Question: What is the primary reason for the terminological confusion surrounding the terms "mental health" and "mental illness"?
A) Lack of research in the field of mental health
B) The terms are often used interchangeably in everyday language
C) The absence of a widely agreed consensus on the meaning of these terms
D) Insufficient funding for mental health initiatives
Correct Answer: C)

## MCQ 2
Question: According to the World Health Organization (WHO), what is the definition of positive mental health?
A) The absence of mental illness
B) The presence of mental illness
C) A state of well-being characterized by a sense of purpose, quality relationships, and self-respect
D) A state of emotional distress
Correct Answer: C)

## MCQ 3
Question: What is the estimated prevalence of mental health problems among adolescents in the UK?
A) 1 in 10
B) 1 in 20
C) 1 in 50
D) 1 in 100
Correct Answer: B)

## MCQ 4
Question: What is a possible explanation for the stigmatization of mental illness?
A) The belief that mental illness is contagious
B) The perception that people with mental illness are dangerous and unpredictable
C) The idea that mental illness is a sign of weakness
D) The notion that mental illness is a result of poor parenting
Correct Answer: B)

## MCQ 5
Question: What is a potential consequence of stigmatization of mental illness?
A) Increased willingness to seek help
B) Improved mental health outcomes
C) Increased feelings of shame and social isolation
D) Reduced symptoms of mental illness
Correct Answer: C)
